# DashDrive City Launch Playbook (90-Day Strategy)

This playbook outlines the exact step-by-step strategy for launching DashDrive in a new city, focused on achieving the **Network Flywheel** within the first 90 days.

---

## 🏁 Phase 1: Pre-Launch & Seeding (Day 1–15)
**Goal**: Secure critical supply density before opening to riders.

1. **Strategic Hub Selection**: Identify a high-traffic "Anchor District" (e.g., Central Business District or Airport). Do NOT launch city-wide.
2. **Driver Onboarding**: Recruited and verify the first 50–100 "Founding Drivers."
3. **Hardware & Training**: Physical branding (stickers), app training, and standard operating procedures.
4. **Platform Setup**: Configure city boundaries (H3 resolution 7) and localized pricing in the `Pricing Engine`.

---

## 🚀 Phase 2: Soft Launch (Day 16–45)
**Goal**: Test Liquidity and stabilize Driver Utilization.

1. **Targeted Rider Acquisition**: Invite early adopters specifically in the "Anchor District."
2. **Utilization Monitoring**: Target >40% Utilization. Use the `Earnings Optimizer` to subsidize low-trip hours to prevent churn.
3. **Liquidity Balancing**: Activate **Launch Mode** in the `Dispatch Brain` to prioritize tight clusters over wide-radius dispatch.
4. **Service Stacking Intro**: Launch `Parcel Delivery` on Day 30 to increase driver job frequency.

---

## 📈 Phase 3: Growth & Expansion (Day 46–75)
**Goal**: Build Density and trigger the Flywheel.

1. **District Expansion**: Expand into 2–3 neighboring districts once the Anchor District maintains <5m pickup times.
2. **Referral Loops**: Activate rider-to-rider and driver-to-driver referral program in the `Campaign Service`.
3. **Surge Testing**: Calibrate demand-driven price adjustments.
4. **Food & Mart Launch**: Introduce high-frequency services to maximize the "Network Effect."

---

## 🏆 Phase 4: Market Maturity (Day 76–90)
**Goal**: Profitability and Operational Excellence.

1. **Algorithm Optimization**: Switch from `Launch Mode` to `Standard Mode` in the Dispatch Brain.
2. **Fraud Hardening**: Tighten thresholds in the `Fraud Detection Engine` as volume grows.
3. **Loyalty Program**: Launch the `DashDrive Premier` tier for top riders and drivers.
4. **City Autonomy**: Transition management to a local Operations Hub.

---
